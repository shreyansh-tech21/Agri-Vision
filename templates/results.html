How the AI Decided

We use Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) to extract gradients from the last convolutional block (layer4[-1] of ResNet50). This represents the highest-level spatial features before classification.

Color Code Interpretation

Red (High Importance): Major infected lesions, symptoms, or pest locations.
Yellow/Green (Medium): Surrounding affected margins or stressed leaf tissue.
Blue (Low Importance): Normal backgrounds, stems, or healthy green foliage ignored by the AI.
